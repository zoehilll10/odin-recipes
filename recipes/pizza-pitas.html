Pita Pizza's

A quick lunch to prepare when you dont know what to do with your leftover pita's

These are simple and quick to prepare.

They are also a fun way to make pizza if you have younger ones around that love to help out in the kitchen.

Ingredients:

  • Pita's
  • Tomato Puree or passata
  • Mozerlla cheese
  • Pepperoni
  • Additional toppings

Steps:

  1. Preheat you overn or airfyer.
  2. Evenly spread tomato puree onto pita
  3. Top with Mozerella cheese
  4. Add perpperoni and any additional toppings you would like.
  5. Bake for 8-10 mins until cheese is melted and enjoy!
